Nzan Ogbe is a serial entrepreneur with over 30 years of experience creating and growing businesses in various sectors, including commercial trading, real estate, oil & gas, and telecommunications.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

With a vast network of contacts in both the public and private sectors In Nigeria, he is also the Chairman of LPV Energies as well as a board member of several corporate organizations, including Macif Holdings, She-Val Engineering Services, and Reed Priceman Limited, amongst others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Nzan holds a Bachelor’s degree in Communication Arts and has attended many courses in diverse areas of interest. He is an alumnus of the prestigious Georgetown Leadership Seminar and is currently pursuing an Executive MSc in Major Programme Management.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
