Bayo Adebiyi has extensive experience in the Oil and Gas industry. He had a full career of over 30 years with Schlumberger Limited, where he held several key positions including Wireline Field Engineer, Field Services Manager, Sales and Commercial Manager, Product Champion, General Manager Operations, Business Development Manager, New Technology Manager, and Corporate Head of Global Accounts of large, medium and small E&P Companies for all Schlumberger’s 16 business segments offerings.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Bayo has managed a multidisciplinary, multicultural team of Directors, Managers, and Geoscientists based in the oil and gas capitals of the world. He has worked and lived on 4 continents and one year prior to joining Schlumberger was spent with Shell as Petroleum Engineer. He also serves as CEO of BlackGEM Limited, a Business and Management Consultancy Company.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Bayo holds a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Obafemi Awolowo University in Nigeria.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
