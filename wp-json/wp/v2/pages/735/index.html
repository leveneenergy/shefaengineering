Yinka Adelodun has over 27 years of professional experience in the Energy Industry. The majority of his career was spent with Schlumberger, a multinational Oil and Gas services company. During his career with Schlumberger, he gained experience in Business Management, Project Management, Service Delivery Management, HR Management, and Product Development. He held key leadership and strategic positions within the company including Field Service Manager, Service Delivery Manager, Operations Manager, VP of Integrated Project Management for Europe and Africa, and VP of Drilling Group for Europe and Africa. He has been responsible for managing multimillion-dollar projects. He worked in multiple countries and continents and the experiences gained from these postings gave him a strong global perspective.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Entrepreneurial passion combined with pragmatic optimism defines Yinka\u2019s outlook in life and business. His current interest revolves around Energy Transition. He has also been actively involved with projects on natural gas, bioenergy, Agri-voltaic, and microgrids.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Yinka holds a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Lagos and an M.Sc. in Sustainable Energy Development from the University of Calgary.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
